Instant Pot Beef Barbacoa

Italian-American dish

Ingredients

  • An Instant Pot or electric pressure cooker (if you’re looking for a slow cooker
    version—try our Slow Cooker Beef Barbacoa)
  • A blender or food processor – For blending the onions, garlic, tomato paste,
    water, and spices to make the sauce.
  • Beef chuck roast or brisket – Chuck roast is often more economical than brisket,
    but you can use whichever one you prefer or have access to (check the bottom of
    your freezer…roasts often hide out down there!).
  • Kosher salt – For rubbing the meat before searing. Coarse or fine sea salt can also
    be used.
  • Yellow onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste – Adds depth and a slight sweetness to balance out the onion and
    spices.
  • Water
  • Fresh lime juice – Adds tartness.
  • Ground cumin
  • Dried oregano
  • Ground cloves – A little goes a long way.
  • Bay leaves
  • Cooking fat of choice – For searing the meat before pressure cooking.
